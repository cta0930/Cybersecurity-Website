Why Should Cyber Hygiene Matter?

We live in a complex and ever evolving digital world. Taking a strong stance on cyber hygiene protects you, your family, and your business from becoming a victim of cyber criminals

What is Cyber Hygiene?

Cyber hygiene is the routine practices that people and organizations employ to maintain system health and overall online security. This includes everything from proper updates to technology you use, backups of data, password management, and more!

Why It's Important

Properly maintaining your devices and accounts is crucial to protect your sensitive data from hackers and breaches. Even small mistakes can lead to risks and harm which is why practicing smart cyber hygiene is so important.
